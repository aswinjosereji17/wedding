Events

27th November

Mehndi 1PM - 5PM

It's one of the oldest traditional Indian pre-wedding ceremonies where the bride and groom attend together. A professional mehndi artist applies mehndi to the bride's hands and feet. The event has a celebratory festival feel with vibrant clothes, dancing, singing, games, and more.

Cocktail Night 7PM - 12AM

This is far from ethnic. The bride & groom want "shots before the knot". With flowing booze and western attire, everyone will be showing off their new dance moves. Expect beer pong matches & karaoke for sure.

28th November

Haldi 10AM - 11AM

The haldi ceremony, held separately for the bride and groom, involves applying turmeric paste to the bride's hands, legs, and face. The groom is not allowed to see the bride after this until the wedding ceremony.

Wedding 6PM - 12AM

The main event where the bride and groom tie the knot. The groom arrives with the barat and is greeted by the bride's side with dhol. Guests attend this event in large numbers as the couple circles the sacred fire and are declared husband and wife.
